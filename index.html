Our Story

Authentic Flavors, Right Here in Bluffton

Mariachi Loco Mexican Grill & Bar has been serving the Bluffton community with bold, homemade Mexican favorites. From sizzling fajitas to hand-crafted burritos, every dish is made with care and fresh ingredients — the way it's meant to be.

We're located in the Harrison Plaza on the south side of town — easy to find, impossible to forget. Whether it's lunch, dinner, or a cold drink at the bar, we've got a seat with your name on it.
